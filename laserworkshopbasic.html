Basic Laser Cutting Workshop

Start your journey with laser cutting technology and learn to create precise, beautiful designs with this powerful fabrication tool.

Register for Workshop →

Overview

Our Basic Laser Cutting Workshop introduces you to the fundamentals of laser cutting technology. In this hands-on course, you'll learn how to design for laser cutting, operate the machine safely, and complete your first laser-cut projects.

Laser cutting is one of the most versatile digital fabrication tools, allowing for precise cutting and engraving on a variety of materials. This workshop provides a comprehensive introduction to this technology, combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand laser cutter technology and its capabilities
  • Learn safety procedures and machine operation
  • Create vector designs suitable for laser cutting
  • Understand material properties and laser settings
  • Complete at least two laser-cut projects
  • Gain confidence in independent machine operation

Workshop Structure

Session 1: Introduction & Design

  • Laser cutting technology overview
  • Safety procedures and machine introduction
  • Creating vector files with Inkscape (free software)
  • Design principles for laser cutting

Session 2: Hands-on Cutting

  • Material selection and preparation
  • Setting up the laser cutter
  • Power and speed settings for different materials
  • Project work: creating personalized designs
  • Troubleshooting common issues

Projects

During this workshop, you'll complete the following projects:

Personal Nameplate

Create a custom nameplate or sign with engraving and cutting techniques.

Interlocking Design

Design and fabricate a 3D object using interlocking flat pieces.

Who is this for?

  • No prior experience with laser cutting required
  • Basic computer skills
  • Interest in digital fabrication and design

Tools & Materials

  • CO2 laser cutter
  • Inkscape (free vector design software)
  • Various materials (wood, acrylic, cardboard)
  • Safety equipment
