Advance Laser Cutting Workshop

Master advanced laser cutting techniques, parametric design, and complex material processing for professional-level fabrication.

Register for Workshop →

Overview

The Advanced Laser Cutting Workshop is designed for those who have mastered the basics and want to take their skills to the next level. This intensive workshop covers advanced techniques, parametric design, material science, and professional workflow optimization.

Through a combination of technical instruction and hands-on projects, you'll develop the expertise to tackle complex designs, work with challenging materials, and optimize your workflow for efficient production. By the end of this workshop, you'll have the skills to create professional-quality laser-cut projects and products.

What You'll Learn

  • Master parametric design for customizable laser-cut projects
  • Learn advanced material processing techniques
  • Create complex joinery and 3D assemblies
  • Understand kerf compensation and precision optimization
  • Integrate laser cutting with other digital fabrication technologies
  • Complete a sophisticated personal project

Session 2: Material Mastery

  • Working with challenging materials (leather, specialty acrylics, composites)
  • Multi-layer cutting and registration techniques
  • Material bending and forming after laser processing
  • Surface treatment and finishing methods

Session 3: Professional Production

  • Optimizing machine settings for speed and quality
  • Material nesting and efficient use of materials
  • Batch production strategies
  • Quality control and testing procedures
  • Integrating laser cutting with other fabrication methods
