Advance 3D Modeling Workshop

Take your 3D modeling skills to the next level with advanced techniques, complex geometries, and professional workflows.

Register Now →

Overview

Advance your 3D modeling skills specifically for 3D printing. This workshop builds on foundational knowledge, focusing on complex techniques, mastering material parameters for key FDM filaments (like PLA, ABS, PETG/PEI), and professional design-for-print workflows.

Designed for those with basic experience, you'll learn to tackle intricate geometries, optimize models for printability, and complete a significant portfolio project, preparing you for sophisticated additive manufacturing challenges.

Learning Outcomes

  • Master advanced modeling techniques and workflows
  • Create complex organic and mechanical shapes
  • Apply texturing and materials to models
  • Export files in the correct format for 3D printing
  • Create assembly-ready components with precise measurements
  • Understanding different parameters for different materials
  • Implement generative design principles

Workshop Structure

Module 1: Advanced Modeling Techniques

  • Non-destructive modeling workflows
  • Boolean operations and optimization
  • Subdivision surface modeling
  • Advanced sculpting techniques

Module 2: Engineering and Precision

  • More advanced modeling techniques
  • Parametric modeling fundamentals
  • Designing for manufacturing constraints
  • Working with precise measurements and tolerances
  • Creating assembly-ready components

Module 3: Optimization and Export

  • Topology optimization for strength and material usage
  • Preparing models for various manufacturing processes
  • Advanced slicing techniques for 3D printing
  • Project work: designing your own object
  • Final project: creating a complex functional model

Prerequisites

  • Basic 3D modeling experience (our Basic Workshop or equivalent)
  • Familiarity with 3D modeling software interface
  • Understanding of basic 3D printing concepts

Tools Used

  • PrusaSlicer 2.9.2 (For parameter control)
  • Fusion360 (User Friendly)
  • TinkerCAD (browser-based alternative)
  • Meshmixer for model preparation and optimization
  • Advanced 3D printers and materials demonstration

Workshop Details

  • Duration: 1 day (3 hours)
  • Location: Your Location
  • Group Size: Minimum 8 ; Maximum 20 participants
  • Cost: €100 (includes materials)
